PRIVACY POLICY

War Council

Last updated: June 5, 2026

1. WHO WE ARE

Aetherra is a tabletop wargame army builder developed and operated by Olivier Hirschi. If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us at aetherra.support@gmail.com.

2. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT

We collect only what is necessary to operate the app:

  • Account data: Your email address when you register or sign in.
  • User-generated content: Armies, army units, custom factions, units, abilities, and any photos you upload as unit portraits.
  • Game data: Game session state (rounds, command points) while a game is in progress.
  • Subscription status: Whether your account has an active premium subscription. Payment details are handled exclusively by Stripe and are never stored by us.

We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or any behavioural/analytics data. We do not use advertising networks.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • To store and sync your armies and custom content across devices.
  • To manage your premium subscription status.
  • To respond to support requests you send us.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. DATA STORAGE & PROCESSORS

Your data is stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure in the EU). Supabase acts as our data processor under a data processing agreement. You can read their privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

Payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc. We receive only a subscription status flag — no card numbers or bank details ever reach our servers. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

5. PHOTOS & UPLOADED IMAGES

Photos you upload as unit portraits are stored in your private Supabase storage bucket. They are accessible only to your authenticated account and are not shared publicly.

6. DATA RETENTION

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting aetherra.support@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at aetherra.support@gmail.com.

8. COOKIES & LOCAL STORAGE

Aetherra uses browser local storage strictly for session management (keeping you signed in). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party cookies.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy from time to time. Check this page periodically for updates. The date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent version.

10. CONTACT

For any privacy-related questions: aetherra.support@gmail.com
